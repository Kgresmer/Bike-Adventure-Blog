About Us

We built this site to keep the world updated on our West Coast Bike Tour! We have quit our jobs and devoted our life to the journey south with nothing but our bikes, camping gear and an open mind. We are using a route from the Adventure Cycling Association and will do our best to avoid highways. The journey is just over a thousand miles! We will be camping most of the time and occasionally using Warmshowers.org and maybe AirBnB. We plan on doing a bike two days, take one day off type schedule for about a month. We will be taking lots of photos and hopefully have some interesting stories to tell and thats what this blog is for. We will try to update it everyday, but lets be honest that may not happen. Stay Tuned!
